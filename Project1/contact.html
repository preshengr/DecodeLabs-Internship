FAQs

Frequently Asked Questions

How long does a typical repair take?

Most smartphone repairs like screen or battery replacements are done within 1–2 hours. Laptop repairs typically take 24–48 hours. Complex motherboard or water damage repairs may take up to 3–5 business days.

Do I need to book an appointment in advance?

Booking in advance via our website ensures you get a guaranteed time slot and avoids waiting. Walk-ins are welcome but subject to technician availability.

Will I lose my data during repair?

For most repairs, your data is not affected. However, we always recommend backing up your device before any repair. If a factory reset is required, we'll notify you first and offer data recovery assistance.

Do you use original parts?

We use genuine OEM or OEM-grade parts for all repairs. We'll always specify the grade of the part and the associated cost before proceeding, so you can make an informed decision.

What payment methods do you accept?

We accept cash, bank transfers (all Nigerian banks), and POS card payments. Full payment is collected upon device collection after repairs are completed.

Is there a warranty on repairs?

Yes. All repairs come with a 30-day workmanship warranty. If the same fault reoccurs within 30 days due to our repair, we'll fix it at no extra charge.
