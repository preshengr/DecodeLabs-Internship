Smart Tech Limited was founded in Abuja with one mission: to provide fast, honest, and high-quality gadget repair services to individuals and businesses across FCT.

✅ 7+ Years in Business
Our Story

From a Small Workshop to Abuja's Trusted Repair Centre

Smart Tech Limited started in 2018 as a one-room workshop in Wuse Zone 6, driven by a passion for technology and a commitment to honest repair. What began as a small operation handling a few phone repairs a week has grown into a full-service gadget repair centre serving thousands of clients across the FCT.

Today, our team of certified technicians handles everything from cracked screens to complex motherboard-level diagnostics — always using quality parts and delivering transparent pricing before any work begins.
